Spicy Chicken Popcorn

Calories: 185 per serving C:15gr P:23gr F:4gr

PREP TIME: 30mins COOK TIME: 60mins DIFFICULTY: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2kg Boneless Chicken Breast
  • 250gr Panko
  • 65gr Tapioca Flour
  • 5gr Cayenne Pepper
  • 5gr Smoked Paprika
  • 10gr Salt
  • 5gr Black Pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50gr Low Calorie Hot Sauce
  • Low Calorie Cooking Spray

Instructions

  1. Chop the chicken into small pieces, about the size of a dice.
  2. Sprinkle some salt and pepper on them.
  3. Put the chicken pieces in a big plastic bag or a bowl.
  4. Add some tapioca flour and shake or stir well to coat the chicken evenly.
  5. In a large bowl, mix the panko with some salt, pepper, cayenne pepper and paprika
  6. In another bowl, break 2 eggs and whisk them with some hot sauce.
  7. Dip the chicken pieces in the egg mixture and then roll them in the panko mixture. If you are using a bag, don’t put too many chicken pieces at once.
  8. Shake the bag to cover the chicken with the panko mixture.
  9. Make sure to let the extra egg drip off the chicken before putting it in the panko mixture. Otherwise, the panko might stick together and make a mess. If that happens, you might need to throw away the mixture and start over with a new batch.
  10. Put the coated chicken pieces on a baking sheet.
  11. Preheat oven to 204°C.
  12. Spray some oil on top of the chicken.
  13. Put the chicken pieces in the oven in one layer for 4-5 minutes.
  14. Turn chicken pieces after 4-5 minutes in the oven.
  15. Place chicken pieces back in the oven for another 4-5 minutes.
  16. Take out the chicken when they are golden brown and cooked through. This usually takes about 10 minutes in the oven.
  17. You might need to cook the chicken in several batches, depending on the size of your oven.
  18. Place cooked chicken in a clean bowl and coat with low calorie hot sauce.
  19. Serve the cooked chicken pieces.
