Protein Cinnamon Rolls

Calories: 324 calories per serving C:49gr P:14gr F:8gr

PREP TIME: 30mins COOK TIME: 10mins DIFFICULTY: Easy

Ingredients

  • 40gr Oat Flour
  • 35gr Tapioca Flour
  • 20gr Vanilla Casein Protein Powder
  • 2gr Baking Powder
  • 80gr Low Fat Milk
  • 30gr Egg Whites
  • 10gr Butter - melted
  • 20gr Sugar
  • 5gr Cinnamon
  • 30gr Whipped Cream Cheese Frosting

Instructions

  1. Set the oven to 204°C or prepare the air fryer.
  2. Combine the flours, baking powder, and protein powder in a bowl. Add the milk and egg whites and mix well to form a dough.
  3. Refrigerate the dough for 10-15 minutes to make it less sticky.
  4. Lay out two large sheets of wax paper and spray them with oil on both sides.
  5. Put the dough between the wax paper sheets and roll it into a ¼" thick rectangle.
  6. In a small bowl, stir the sugar and cinnamon together and melt the butter in a microwave.
  7. Brush the butter over the dough and sprinkle the cinnamon sugar on top.
  8. Cut the dough lengthwise into two equal strips.
  9. Using the wax paper, roll up each strip into a log. Then, join the two logs together by placing one at the end of the other and rolling them into one big log.
  10. Cut the log into two pieces to make two rolls.
  11. Grease a sheet pan, baking dish, or cast iron skillet with oil and place the rolls on it.
  12. Bake the rolls in the oven for 8-12 minutes or in the air fryer for 6 minutes, until they are lightly browned on top.
  13. Drizzle the frosting over the rolls. Enjoy your two large rolls.
