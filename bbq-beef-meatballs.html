BBQ Beef Meatballs

Calories: 995 per serving C:12gr P:20gr F:3gr

PREP TIME: 20mins COOK TIME: 40mins DIFFICULTY: Easy

Ingredients

  • 850gr Lean Ground Beef
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 50gr Oat Flour
  • 6gr Garlic Powder
  • 6gr Onion Powder
  • 6gr Salt
  • 6gr Pepper
  • 40gr Low Calorie BBQ Sauce

Instructions

  1. Combine beef, eggs, oat flour, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, and BBQ sauce in a large bowl and blend well. - If you do not have oat flour, you can substitute it with whole oats or breadcrumbs.
  2. Coat a sheet pan with oil and use a 30g cookie scoop to shape the meatballs and place them on the pan. Leave some space between each meatball and use another pan if necessary. - This recipe should get you about 30 meatballs.
  3. Rub some oil on your hands.
  4. Roll the meatballs gently to make them smooth and firm.
