Peanut Butter Protein Granola

Calories: 270 calories per serving C:34gr P:12gr F:9gr

PREP TIME: 7mins COOK TIME: 20mins DIFFICULTY: Easy

Ingredients

  • 350gr Oats
  • 150gr Peanut Butter
  • 140gr Maple Syrup
  • 5gr Vanilla Extract
  • 60gr Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 5gr Cinnamon Powder
  • 5gr Salt

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 180 °C.
  2. Put the peanut butter, maple syrup, and vanilla in a bowl that can go in the microwave.
  3. Heat it until the peanut butter melts.
  4. Add the cinnamon, protein powder, and salt to the same bowl and mix well.
  5. In another large bowl, combine the oats and the peanut butter/syrup mixture. Make sure the oats are evenly coated.
  6. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper and spread the oat mixture on it in a thin layer of about ½" thick.
  7. Bake the oat mixture for 20-22 minutes or until it is slightly firm in the middle.
  8. Let the oat mixture cool down completely and then break it into chunks of your preferred size.
  9. Keep the granola in a container that is sealed tightly.
  10. Weigh the total amount of granola you made and divide it by 10 to get the weight of one serving.
